A company of visionaries.

    Our mission

    We’re on a mission to open city skies by accelerating a new zero-carbon mobility paradigm – electric air vehicles that operate with an unrelenting focus on safety, customer needs, and ground-to-air connectivity.

    In order to reach its full potential, AAM must be integrated into existing transportation networks. That’s why Supernal is collaborating with public transportation agencies and policymakers to work out the details of the complete ground-to-air ecosystem. Through it all, we’re insistent that our solution be an open system, not a proprietary network. We know our ultimate success depends on the ingenuity and dedication of others. Air mobility will be a collaborative achievement.

    Strategically positioned.

    Washington, D.C.

    Dedicated to ensuring our meet strict U.S. aviation standards, Supernal’s policy and commercial headquarters is located in the heart of Washington D.C., just a few blocks from the White House.
